LIPLEY IMAGES & LOGOS
======================

Place your brand images in this folder to replace the placeholder graphics on the website.

Expected Files:
1. "logo.png" - Official brand logo (transparent background recommended).
2. "rose-balm.png" - Product image for "Lipley Rose Lip Balm".
3. "strawberry-balm.png" - Product image for "Lipley Strawberry Lip Balm".
4. "hero-product.png" - Product image for the Hero Section highlight.

By default, the website uses beautiful inline CSS/SVG fallback layouts. As soon as you upload the PNG files above into this folder, the website will automatically load them instead of the placeholders.
